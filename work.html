TangoChan

A Ruby on Rails web app to assist language learners practice Japanese vocabulary. Users can view and copy other users' lists, practice pre-made lists, befriend other users, and create their own lists from an extensive Japanese dictionary. Users can practice those words in a flashcard format that tracks their correct and incorrect answers. To better understand the modern context of a word users can get the latest tweet that mentions it.
